Grilled Chicken Wings

Ingredients

1. ⅔ cup all-purpose flour
2. 4 tablespoons paprika
3. 2 teaspoons garlic powder
4. 2 teaspoons ground black pepper
5. 20 chicken wings flats and drumettes, separated
6. Dipping sauce for serving
7. Fresh parsley for garnish

Taco Dip

Ingredients

1. 8 ounces cream cheese room temperature (1 brick)
2. ¼ cup salsa store-bought or homemade
3. 2 cups chopped iceberg lettuce (about ½ head)
4. 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
5. 1 cup chopped tomatoes
6. 2 tablespoons taco sauce optional, for topping
7. Fresh parsley for garnish

Pico de Gallo

Ingredients

1. ⅔ cup all-purpose flour
2. ½ teaspoon kosher salt
3. ¼ cup white onion diced
4. 1 serrano pepper or jalapeño pepper, minced (for less heat, remove the seeds and membranes)
5. ⅓ cup finely chopped fresh cilantro leaves
6. 1 tablespoon freshly squeezed lime juice from 1 lime

Parmesan Roasted Potatoes

Ingredients

1. 3 tablespoons salted butter melted (⅜ stick)
2. ½ cup Italian-style Panko breadcrumbs
3. ½ cup pre-grated Parmesan cheese
4. ¼ cup freshly shredded Parmesan cheese
5. 1.5 pounds baby red potatoes halved lengthwise (1 bag)
6. Minced fresh parsley optional, for garnish
